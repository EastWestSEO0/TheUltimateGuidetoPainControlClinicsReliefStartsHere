Understanding Neuropathy and Nerve Pain

Neuropathy is a condition caused by damage to the peripheral nerves, leading to symptoms such as:

Tingling or prickling sensations in the feet or hands

Burning pain in the feet

Numbness or loss of sensation in the legs

Muscle weakness and coordination difficulties
